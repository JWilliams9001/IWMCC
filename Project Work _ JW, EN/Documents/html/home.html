The Ian Williams Motocross Collection: A Digital Transcription

This website is home to the digitised version of a scrapbook created by the late Ian Williams (left), detailing his time as a motocross competitor during the 1950s in Ireland.

Ian, along with his brother, Eric, were well known in the Dublin motocross circles and featured in numerous newspaper articles, a selection of which are presented here. Our aims for this project are to preserve a piece of family history and to contribute to the sporting and cultural heritage of Ireland.
